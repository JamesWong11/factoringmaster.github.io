Our Vision

Our goal is to help students in need of a better education. We would like to target high schoolers who are in ninth through twelth grade. However, this site and its application shall be freely used to help anyone. You might even learn a thing or two!

Factoring can be used in many different scenarios. In real life, factoring can be used to calculate the dimensions of a pool and the area of large-sized buildings.

While there is only a small percentage of factoring questions on the SAT Math section, they tend to be more difficult than others. That is why it is important to refine your factoring skills before test taking!

This application serves as a way to get your mind off of external stress. Enjoy learning with puzzling factoring questions that are also engaging and fun!

Struggling with learning at home and in school? This website is designed to be with you every step of the way! There are step-by-step solutions that tell you exactly why any answer is wrong or right in the context of the problem.
